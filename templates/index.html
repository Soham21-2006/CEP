About Us

Welcome to CampusLost&Found - your trusted platform for reuniting lost items with their owners across our college campus.

We understand how frustrating it can be to lose valuable items. Our mission is to create a seamless bridge between those who have lost something and those who have found something, making our campus a more connected and helpful community.
