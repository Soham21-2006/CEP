✨ About Our Mission

At CampusLost&Found, we believe no belonging should stay lost forever. Our platform digitally connects students, faculty, and staff to report lost/found items instantly, reducing waste and fostering a caring campus environment.

🌟 Over 1000+ items reunited since launch. Secure, reliable, and built for campus communities.

💡 Core Features

🔐

User Registration & Authentication

Secure signup with email verification, roll number validation, and encrypted password storage. Only verified campus members can access the platform.

🛡️

Secure Claiming Mechanism

Multi-step verification process for claiming found items. Proof submission, OTP confirmation, and tamper-proof claim logs ensure safe returns.

📊

Admin Dashboard

Powerful analytics, user management, item moderation, and real-time campus recovery insights for administrators and moderators.
